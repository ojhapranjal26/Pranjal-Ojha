Class Management System

The Tuition Management System is a comprehensive web application designed to streamline and automate the operations of a tuition center. This system efficiently manages various aspects of student and teacher data, including student marks, results, fee tracking, and attendance.

Key Features:
  • Student Data Management: Manage student details, marks, and results.
  • Fee Management: Track fees, generate receipts, and manage installments.
  • Results Management: Store and display students' exam results securely.
  • Teacher Attendance & Salary Management: Track attendance, calculate salaries, and generate salary slips.
  • Salary Slip Generation: Print or share salary slips digitally for teachers.
  • Student Portal: A secure portal for students to view their data, marks, results, and fees.
  • Teacher Portal: A portal for teachers to access their attendance and salary information.

This system enhances the efficiency of tuition center operations, simplifies administration tasks, and provides a seamless experience for both students and teachers. It's designed with a user-friendly interface to ensure ease of use.
